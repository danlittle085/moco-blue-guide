Please Read Carefully

House Rules

This is our family home and we kindly ask that you treat it with the same care and respect as your own.

  • Keep noise to a minimum, especially outside and between 10 pm – 8 am.
  • No parties without host permission - extra cleaning charges or immediate vacation may result.
  • Smoking inside is strictly forbidden - minimum 5,000 THB deep-clean charge applies.
  • Outside smokers must use an ashtray and dispose of butts in the waste bin once extinguished.
  • Suspected use or possession of illegal drugs will result in authorities being engaged.
  • No glass or bottles in the pool area - plastic glasses are provided.
  • Pool maintenance visits once or twice a week. Report issues immediately; do not attempt to self-fix.
  • Children and infants must be under active supervision at all times.
  • Park cars and motorbikes outside the gate only.
  • Do not drive inside the gate - underground water pipes can be damaged.
  • Guests are responsible for any damage to the garden and/or underground pipes.
  • Do not rearrange the furniture.
  • Lock all windows and doors when leaving.
  • Turn off all appliances when not in use - A/C, fans, hair straighteners, chargers, etc.
  • No shoes inside the house.
  • No pets allowed.
  • Reset the safe before checkout.
  • Power: outages are common due to frequent maintenance work and Koh Samui's remote location. If we have information regarding planned outages we will notify you. We cannot be held accountable for power outages.
  • Water: is a precious commodity on Koh Samui, so please be mindful of your usage. The house receives water only twice a week - usually Wednesday and Friday. We have a large water tank, but if it is emptied there will be a one-off charge of 600 baht to have it refilled.
  • Toilet: the narrow waste pipes on Koh Samui are not designed to withstand man-made disposals. We kindly ask that you put all paper products into the toilet bin provided to prevent blockages and flooding.
  • A/C: Koh Samui is HOT and we understand that the air con is necessary. We ask that guests ensure all doors and windows are closed when running, are mindful of their usage, and ensure all units are turned off when going out.
  • Insects: mosquitos, geckos, ants, etc are all resident on Koh Samui. Keep doors closed at dawn and dusk, and do not leave food out as this will attract insects.

Housekeeping is available at 1,000 THB per session - please just ask.

Our Favourites

Island Guide

🛕 Big Buddha (Wat Phra Yai) Map

One of Koh Samui's most famous landmarks, featuring a 12-metre golden Buddha statue overlooking the sea. A must-visit cultural and religious site.

🐉 Guan Yu Koh Samui Shrine Map

An impressive Chinese shrine centred around a giant statue of Guan Yu, the Chinese god of loyalty and bravery. A unique cultural attraction on the island.

⛩️ Wat Plai Laem Map

A colourful lakeside temple complex known for its striking 18-armed Guanyin statue, ornate architecture, and peaceful surroundings.

🪨 Grandmother & Grandfather Rocks (Hin Ta & Hin Yai) Map

Koh Samui's most famous natural rock formations, shaped by centuries of erosion and accompanied by local legends.

📸 Overlap Stone 2 Map

A remarkable natural balancing rock offering panoramic views across the island and coastline. One of Samui's most impressive viewpoints.

💦 Na Muang Waterfalls (1 & 2) Map

A pair of scenic waterfalls surrounded by jungle in the island's interior. Na Muang 1 is easily accessible, while Na Muang 2 rewards a short hike with beautiful views and a more secluded setting.

🍹 Fisherman's Village Map

A charming seaside district with boutique shops, cafés, restaurants, and bars. Particularly lively in the evenings and during the weekly Friday Night Market.

🏖️ Choeng Mon Beach best all round Map

A beautiful, sheltered bay with soft sand and calm water, making it ideal for families and swimming. Home to several beach clubs and a more relaxed atmosphere. Approx. 10 minutes by car; parking is generally easy, with several roadside and beachfront parking areas.

🍹 Chaweng Beach best atmosphere Map

Koh Samui's most famous beach, offering a long stretch of white sand, clear water, beach bars, water sports, and plenty of restaurants. Approx. 5 minutes by car; parking can be difficult, but most beach clubs and some restaurants offer parking for customers.

🤿 Crystal Beach best snorkelling Map

A small picturesque cove with crystal-clear water, excellent swimming, and some of the island's best snorkelling close to shore. Approx. 20 minutes by car; parking is limited, so arriving earlier in the day is recommended.

🚶 Lamai Beach best beach walks Map

A long sandy beach offering a great balance of relaxation and amenities. Less busy than Chaweng but with plenty of beachfront restaurants, bars, and shops. Approx. 25 minutes by car; parking is generally straightforward, with numerous access points along the beach road.

🌅 Lipa Noi Beach best sunsets Map

One of Koh Samui's quietest beaches, known for its shallow water, peaceful atmosphere, and spectacular sunsets. A perfect choice for a relaxed afternoon and evening. Approx. 40 minutes by car; parking is easy, with plenty of space near the beachfront restaurants and resorts.

🚜 Quad Biking

Explore jungle trails, viewpoints, and hidden parts of the island on an exciting off-road adventure.

🌲 Zip Lining

Soar through the rainforest canopy and enjoy spectacular views of Koh Samui from above.

⛳ Golf at Santiburi Samui Country Club Map

Enjoy a round at Santiburi, one of Thailand's most scenic golf courses, set amongst coconut groves and rolling hills.

🎣 Fishing Map

Try your hand at freshwater fishing in a relaxed tropical setting. Suitable for all experience levels, with equipment available and a café on site for refreshments.

🐷 Pig Island Day Trip (Koh Mat Sum) Map

Visit the famous island where friendly pigs roam the beach, with opportunities for swimming, snorkelling, and relaxing by the sea.

🏝️ Ang Thong National Marine Park Map

Explore a stunning archipelago of limestone islands, emerald lagoons, hidden beaches, and spectacular viewpoints.

🐘 Elephant Sanctuary Visit

Spend time with rescued elephants in an ethical sanctuary environment, learning about their care and conservation.
